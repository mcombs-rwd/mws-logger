Applications may request historical rain data via the API.

/rain-api
GET— Entry to API
?meter=
GET— May be one of: green_roof, rain_gauge
&resolution=
GET— May be one of: hour, day, month
&start=
GET— dd-mm-yy hh:mm, will be rounded to nearest period
&stop=
GET— dd-mm-yy hh:mm, will be rounded to nearest period, defaults to now.
&format=
GET— May be one of: json, xml, csv. Defaults to csv.

Examples

For green_roof, from a week ago to now, by day, as CSV

GET rain-api?meter=green_roof&resolution=day&start=2024-03-23

JSON

GET rain-api?meter=green_roof&resolution=day&start=2024-03-23&stop=2024-03-30&format=json
